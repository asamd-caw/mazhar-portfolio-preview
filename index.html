Profile

For two decades, counsel to individuals, institutions and the State in disputes where the outcome cannot be left to chance.

Mazhar Hussain practises across the Supreme Court of India, the High Courts and major tribunals, with a litigation practice spanning commercial disputes, constitutional challenges, white-collar defence and regulatory matters. He is known for measured strategy, rigorous preparation and a calm presence in the courtroom.

He advises companies, public bodies and private clients — appearing at every stage from trial to final appeal, and increasingly as counsel and arbitrator in domestic and institutional arbitration.
